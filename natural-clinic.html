Natural Clinic

Branding Design

Natural Clinic is an organically motivated massage and beauty Spa. They use chemical-free and natural skincare products for both men and women for all treatments.

The final logo design offers simplicity and balance of both organic and natural design elements creating the letter “N”.

The stylized icon also created a flower pattern which played a key role as the secondary/major design element in its branding.
